Raspberries!!

The raspberry likely originated in North Eurasia. Long prized by humans throughout history, raspberries were quickly spread to suitable cooler climates far and wide. Depending on variety, raspberries can bear fruit in 1 or 2 years and then continue to bless us with deliciousness for more than 15 years!

Tips from Our Garden to Yours

Great Variety(ies):

Figure out your chill hours, plant hardiness zone, soil type, etc... and then learn all you can about which strawberries will thrive in your yard. Sequoia and Chandler have done very well in one of our sunny areas, while we are considering Alpine for some of our shadier areas. Still so much to learn...

Great Spot(s):

Identify the amount of sunlight/shade, the exposure/protection from wind and elements, and any other factors pro and con for each spot in your yard. As much as possible, match your great variety(ies) with your great spot(s).
