Blueberries!

The blueberry is a North American original. Breeding the Northern Highbush with the Rabbiteye produced the Southern Highbush. Some varieties of the Southern Highbush have a very low chill hour requirement and can thrive in Southern California pumping out blueberries for as long as 50 years!

Tips from Our Garden to Yours

Great Variety(ies):

Figure out your chill hours, plant hardiness zone, soil type, etc... and then go learn all you can about which blueberries will thrive in your yard. Jewel, Emerald, and Sunshine Blue love our sunny backyard, while O'Neal has been less than stellar. In the future, maybe Misty, Gulf Coast, and Sharpblue will love our sunny frontyard!

Great Spot(s):

Identify the amount of sunlight/shade, the exposure/protection from wind and elements, and any other factors pro and con for each spot in your yard. As much as possible, match your great variety(ies) with your great spot(s).

Great Gardener(s):

Improve the soil, improve the spot (trellis), water the right amount (automatically), etc...
